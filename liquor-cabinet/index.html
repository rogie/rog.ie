The Liquor Cabinet

So many of our social evenings are filled with food, drinks, and conversation, and often, me, mixing some tasty drinks or pouring a finger of bourbon for my guests.

I had never owned a liquor cabinet. Often, I would just find an area to cram some bottles in, but it was time. So many liquor cabinets focus on the liquor itself as the focal point. To me, I'd much rather something beautiful to look at that showcases craft and design, than a statement like "look at my expensive booze."

I've always loved wood. In earlier versions of my websites, I had illustrated wood grain adorning my navigation and button elements. I've poured over furniture websites, admiring the craft of woodworking, but never quite taken the leap to building my own.

Quentin shares this love of beautiful wooden objects and craft, but at a much more obsessive level. As he's grown in his craft, I've wanted to have some of his work in my home.
